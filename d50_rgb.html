Featuring SMD5050 RGB high brightness LED’s, the unique D50 globe lamp has a look similar to a round decorative light bulb, The polycarbonate globe snaps on to its companion base which contains the LED’s. The base can mount into practically any substrate after drilling a 1.2”hole. All wires are concealed behind the substrate.
