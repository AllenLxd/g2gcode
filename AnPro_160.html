G2G's AnPro LED modules provide a 155° wide viewing angle. Because of it's high lumen output, the AnPro helps you create brighter and more evenly illuminated sign using less product. Bringing you more flexibility in installation, the AnPro makes custom sign designs simpler than ever before.
